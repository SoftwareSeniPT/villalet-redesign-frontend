Villa On Top of The Waves

Considered as one of Bali's most luxurious holiday villas, this beachfront super villa is located a few kilometers north of Legian and has been featured numerous times in editorial coverages of fashion and lifestyle publications from all over the world. From its picturesque land and seascape to its cozy yet modern interior, this villa is the epitome of a perfect holiday getaway.

This private villa has a two-storey main building that houses three bedrooms, with the master suite having a large sunken bathtub, a dressing area, an open-air bathroom and access to a private plunge pool. The partially open dining area is connected to a fully-equipped kitchen by a narrow bridge over a small lotus pond which is also located in the main building.
